Yusha’s Storyboard !

Our company goals, values, and who we are :)

A new animation community!

Imagine living in a world without your favorite cartoons when you were growing up. These cartoons have unknowingly shaped us, influenced our humor, and made our childhood more vibrant.

Unfortunately, the animation industry has not been supportive where on average 90% of animators quit their jobs after 3 years in the industry.

Here at Yusha, we want to support animators and welcome new creators to the animation community. With a large technological effort, we hope to ease the tedious tasks in animation, reduce the learning curves, and rebuild the industry that has set the path for so many smiles and giggles.

Everyone has a story to tell ✨

Animation is a large industry. There is a clear inequality where individual creators are outshined by well known animation production studios.

The animation industry is projected to continuously grow and double by 2030, current animators are faced with labor intensive projects, lack of resources, long wait times, and a high risk of product failure. Other AI software companies have tried to do what we’re doing but have ended up participating in the indifferences.

Yusha promises to support all artists from professionals to students and open the doors of the gatekept industry. 

Our Mission

To remove the barrier of entry into the animation space to allow individual creators to tell their otherwise unheard stories.
